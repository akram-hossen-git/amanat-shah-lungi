130 Years

Family legacy business

Amanat Shah Lungi, a proud heritage brand under Amanat Shah Group and a concern of Helal and Brothers, is Bangladesh’s No.1 lungi manufacturer, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern quality. With over 130 years of legacy, the brand is known for its premium cotton and blended fabrics, offering exceptional comfort, durability, and intricate designs. Available in diverse patterns and luxury categories, Amanat Shah Lungi is trusted by generations and continues to make a strong mark in both local and international markets.
